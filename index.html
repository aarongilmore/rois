Rois

RÓIS is an Irish composer, multi-instrumentalist and performer whose songs explore diverse modes of expression; forging an idiosyncratic blend of folk and sean-nós, electronics, jazz harmony and extended vocal techniques.

Often compared to the likes of Meredith Monk, Björk or Hatis Noit, Róis is continually adapting her set list which people have described as transcendent and unearthly.
